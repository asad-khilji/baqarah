Surah Al Baqarah

Ayah 1 & 2:

Possibility 1: Alif Laam Meem. That is the book! There is absolutely no doubt in it whatsoever! (amazing) guidance is for the cautious!

Possibility 2: Alif Laam Meem…That is the book! There isn’t any shred of doubt! In it lies guidance for the cautious.

Possibility 3: Alif Laam Meem is that book, there is absolutely no doubt in it. An incredible guidance for the cautious!

Possibility 4: Alif Laam Meem. That book, there is no doubt about it whatsoever, is guidance for the cautious.

Possibility 5: Alif Laam Meem is that book. There is absolutely no doubt in it as guidance for the cautious.

Possibility 6: Alif Laam Meem. That is the book, without any shred of doubt about it, that serves as guidance for the cautious.

Ayah 3:

Possibility 1: Those who believe in the unseen, and establish prayer, and, only out of whatever We provided them, they spend.

Possibility 2: Who believe in the unseen, and establish prayer, and, only out of whatever We provided them, they spend.
